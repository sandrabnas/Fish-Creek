Our Services

Our doors are open and a veterinarian is available to see your pet for all its medical needs 24 hours a day, 7 days a week! In addition to extending these hours to better accomodate you and your family, there are no ADDITIONAL emergency fees. While most emergency clinics charge up to $95 for emergency visits, we will continue to charge our normal office fee to keep your wallets (and your pets) happy!

Dental work

We offer digital dental x-rays and teeth cleaning. Our skilled Fish Creek team will perform an oral examination, and the findings will serve as the preliminary anesthetic or non-anesthetic dental treatment plan.

Microchip

A microchip is about the size of a grain of rice and is encoded with a unique ID number that will be assigned to your pet. The microchip is placed between your dog’s or cat’s shoulder blades under a veterinarian’s supervision. Implantation is quick, easy and virtually painless.

Pet Grooming

We offer a full service grooming salon called The Pet Coiffure. The Pet Coiffure is open Monday through Friday and our groomer can work with all breeds. All grooming is done under Veterinary supervision.

Urgent Care

We have a veterinarian and personnel on duty 6 days a week who are trained and equipped to handle any urgent care your pet has.

Routine Exams

A physical examination involves observing the general appearance of the dog, listening to the chest with a stethoscope ("auscultation"), and "palpation", or feeling specific areas of the body.

Day care

Each day's agenda includes hours of playtime for your pet, including social time with other dogs. Here, there's no need for a dog walker! We have multiple spacious areas that give your dog the freedom to run and play.
